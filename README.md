# DOPAMINE DIGITAL TRACKER
### One Life Protocol · Daily Habit System

---

## What This Is

The Dopamine Digital Tracker is a self-contained web app for breaking phone addiction through a structured 6-stage behavioral protocol. No backend. No account. No ads. No subscriptions. One HTML file that runs in any browser and stores all data locally on your device.

This is a tool built on the premise that your attention is the most valuable thing you own — and that most of the apps on your phone were designed to steal it.

---

## Before You Use the Tracker

**Read the Protocol document first.**

The tracker is the daily execution tool. The protocol is the context. Without reading the protocol, the habits are just a list of checkboxes without meaning. Open `dopamine_digital_protocol.html` before you use the tracker for the first time.

## How to Use It

### On Desktop (Chrome, Safari, Firefox)
Open `dopamine_digital_tracker_v4.html` directly in any browser. Bookmark it.

### On Mobile (Recommended — iOS Safari)
1. Go to the GitHub Pages URL for this project
2. In Safari, tap the **Share** button (box with arrow)
3. Tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. Give it a name and tap **Add**

The tracker now appears as an icon on your home screen and opens full-screen like a native app.

### On Mobile (Android Chrome)
1. Open the GitHub Pages URL in Chrome
2. Tap the three-dot menu
3. Tap **"Add to Home Screen"**

## The One Rule

**Open the tracker every evening to close the day.**

The app syncs to midnight. When you open it after midnight, the previous day is automatically archived with whatever completion you had. If you did not open the app before midnight, that day is recorded as missed — no backfill, no exceptions.

This is intentional. The nightly opening is itself a habit. It is the anchor of the evening ritual.

**There is no "undo" for a missed day.**

---

## Streak Insurance Tokens

Tokens are the only safety net in the system.

- Earn 1 token every **7 consecutive perfect days** (all habits checked)
- Maximum of **5 tokens** can be banked at once
- When you miss a day, 1 token is **automatically used** to protect your streak — you don't have to do anything
- If you have no tokens, the streak breaks. No exceptions.

Tokens must be earned through discipline. They cannot be purchased, generated, or manipulated.

---

## Data Storage Warning

All your data — XP, streaks, stage progress, badges, history — lives in your browser's `localStorage` on the device you use.

**This means:**
- Do NOT use Private / Incognito mode — data will not be saved
- Do NOT clear your browser's site data — you will lose everything
- Do NOT switch browsers (Chrome → Safari) — they have separate storage
- If you change devices, your data does not transfer automatically

**Recommendation:** Always use the same browser on the same device. If you need to transfer data in the future, a JSON export feature will be added.

## Priority System

Each habit has a priority level that determines its impact:

- **P1** — Non-negotiable. Missing these breaks your day.
- **P2** — High value. Should be completed most days.
- **P3** — Supporting habits. Do them when possible.
- **P4** — Supplementary. Bonus XP.

## Weekly Boss Fights (Stage 5+)

Boss fights unlock at Stage 5 and rotate weekly. Each boss is a 7-day challenge that operates on the honor system — this entire protocol is honor system. Defeating a boss awards a large XP reward and advances to the next mission.

6 bosses cycle repeatedly. Each cycle gets harder in intention, not in rules.

## The Evening Ritual

Every stage has a set of 3 journal questions. These are shown in the tracker but answered on paper — pen and notebook. The tracker records that you did the journal, not what you wrote.

The questions change at each stage. They are specifically designed to surface the psychological patterns that the protocol targets.

**Sunday evenings:** Complete the Weekly Recap. Read the full week of journal entries first, then write the recap. +30 XP.
